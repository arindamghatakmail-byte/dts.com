Our Aim

A Forum of Sports and Culture — Dihibaliharpur Tarun Sangha exists to bring the village together through welfare work, festival tradition, and youth development.

"Let us move forward with unity to build a brighter, culturally enriched community."

— Mr. Chandan Roy, President, FY 2022–23 (Presidential Address)

"Let us move forward with unity to turn our club premises into a vibrant center for public welfare."

— Mr. Ranjit Bag, President, FY 2025–26 (Presidential Address)

What we work toward

Social Welfare

Clothing drives, festive food distribution, and direct support for economically vulnerable families in the village.

Cultural Preservation

Sustaining Durga Puja, Saraswati Puja, Basanta Utsav, and Kobi Pronam as living community traditions.

Civic & Patriotic Life

Marking Republic Day, Independence Day, and Netaji's birthday with flag hoisting and youth participation.

Public Health & Environment

Blood donation camps, tree plantation drives, and local cleanliness campaigns.

Youth & Education

Educational materials, periodicals, and magazines for the youth wing's reading habits.

Permanent Infrastructure

Empowering our society through a future Community Library.

Events & Observances

The recurring calendar that shapes the Sangha's year — cultural, civic, and public-health events drawn from four years of annual reports.

Autumn · Flagship

Durga Puja

The club's largest celebration each year, bringing together hundreds of families for a community pandal.

Winter/Spring

Saraswati Puja

Honouring the goddess of knowledge, with strong participation from village youth.

Spring

Basanta Utsav

Welcoming spring with colour, music, and community gathering.

Cultural

Kobi Pronam

A literary tribute celebrating poets and cultural heritage.

26th January

Republic Day

Flag hoisting and cultural programs promoting civic values among local children.

15th August

Independence Day

Full-honours flag hoisting, national anthem, and youth events.

23rd January

Netaji Jayanti

Observing Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose's birthday with formal ceremony.

Public Health

Blood Donation Camp

Planned and hosted to support community health needs.

Environment

Tree Plantation Day

Village greening drives held on National Tree Plantation Day each year.
